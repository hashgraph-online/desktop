# Privacy Policy
**Last updated: 08/10/2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Hashgraph Online DAO LLC ("Company", "we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our conversational agent application and related services ("Service").

Your privacy is important to us. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information and how we treat it.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Local Application Data
Our desktop application stores configuration data locally on your device, including:
- **API Keys**: OpenAI, Anthropic, and other service credentials
- **Hedera Configuration**: Account ID, private keys, and network preferences
- **Application Settings**: Theme preferences, operational modes, and user preferences
- **Chat History**: Conversation data with AI agents (stored locally only)
- **Legal Acceptance Records**: Records of your acceptance of terms and privacy policy

### Blockchain Data
When you interact with the Hedera network through our application:
- **Transaction Data**: All blockchain transactions are publicly recorded on the Hedera ledger
- **HCS Messages**: Messages sent through Hedera Consensus Service are publicly visible
- **Account Information**: Hedera account IDs and transaction history are publicly accessible
- **Points Data**: HCS-10 and HCS-20 protocol data is permanently recorded on the blockchain

### Third-Party Service Data
When you use integrated AI services:
- **API Usage**: Your queries and responses are processed by third-party AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic)
- **Service Logs**: Third-party services may maintain their own usage logs per their privacy policies

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide Core Functionality**: Enable AI agent interactions and blockchain operations
- **Maintain Configuration**: Store and restore your application settings
- **Enable Features**: Support autonomous mode, manual transaction signing, and other features
- **Legal Compliance**: Track acceptance of legal agreements as required

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Encryption**: Sensitive data like private keys and API keys are encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- **Access Control**: Data is protected by your device's security measures
- **No Cloud Backup**: We do not automatically backup your local data to cloud services

### Blockchain Storage
- **Immutable Records**: Blockchain data cannot be deleted, modified, or made private once recorded
- **Public Visibility**: All Hedera transactions and HCS messages are publicly accessible
- **Permanent Storage**: Blockchain data persists indefinitely on the network

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Services
We share data with third-party services only as necessary:
- **AI Providers**: Your queries are sent to OpenAI, Anthropic, or other selected AI services
- **Blockchain Networks**: Transaction data is broadcast to the Hedera network
- **No Other Sharing**: We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your data with other parties

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or illegal activities
- Respond to government requests
